Sunscreen 

The Originote

I’ve been using the Originote Ceramella Sunscreen SPF 50 PA++++ for a few weeks, and I’m not that impressed. It feels lightweight, but it leaves my skin a bit sticky, which I don’t like. While it claims to have no white cast, it makes my skin look greasy instead of fresh. I also hoped the Vitamin C would brighten my skin, but I haven’t noticed any changes in my complexion. Overall, it hasn’t met my expectations, and I won’t be keeping it in my routine.

Skin Type: Suitable for normal to oily skin because it is a gel-like texture

What it does:

♡ Sun Protection: This sunscreen protects your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays, helping to prevent sunburn and skin aging.
♡ Moisturizes and Strengthens Skin: It contains ceramides and Tremella mushroom, which help keep your skin hydrated and support its natural barrier, making it healthier.
♡ Brightens Skin: With Vitamin C, it helps brighten your skin and even out your complexion while providing sun protection. 

Key Ingredients:

⚝ Ceramides: These are natural fats that help keep your skin hydrated and protect its outer layer.
⚝ Tremella Mushroom Extract: This ingredient is great for moisturizing your skin and helps it stay plump and soft.
⚝ Vitamin C: A powerful antioxidant that brightens your skin and helps protect it from damage caused by the sun.
⚝ Hybrid Sunscreen: This sunscreen uses both chemical and physical filters to protect your skin without leaving a white residue, making it easy to use on all skin tones.

Twems 

I've been using the Twems Beauty Water Sunscreen for quite a while now—I'm already on my fourth bottle! I started using it because I wanted a sunscreen that wouldn’t just protect but would also feel light and refreshing. This one does exactly that. It’s so easy to apply, and I love how it doesn’t leave any sticky or heavy feeling on my skin. It’s become an essential part of my routine, giving me reliable sun protection without disrupting my other skincare. I feel my skin is better protected and healthier, and I’m thrilled with how it fits so perfectly into my daily regimen!

Skin Type: Suitable for normal to dry skin.

What it does:

♡ Sun Protection: This sunscreen protects your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays, helping to prevent sunburn and premature aging.
♡ Moisturizes and Hydrates: With 8 types of hyaluronic acid, it deeply hydrates your skin, giving it a glowing finish without feeling sticky.
♡ Brightens Skin: Arbutin helps brighten your complexion naturally, leaving your skin with a healthy, radiant glow without any white cast.

Key Ingredients:

⚝ Centella Asiatica: Known for its soothing and moisturizing properties, Centella helps calm sensitive skin, reduce redness, and treat irritation. It’s also packed with antioxidants for skin protection.
⚝ 8 Types of Hyaluronic Acid: Provides moisture to the skin, keeping it hydrated and soft, while delivering a glowy finish without any stickiness.
⚝ Arbutin: A natural skin brightener that evens out your skin tone without leaving a white cast, giving you a more radiant complexion.
⚝ Hybrid Sunscreen: This sunscreen combines chemical and physical filters for effective sun protection, offering a lightweight texture that works for all skin tones.
