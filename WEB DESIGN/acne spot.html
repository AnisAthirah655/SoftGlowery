Acne Spot 

Lion Pair 

I used this Lion Pair Acne Spot Treatment because I had persistent breakouts, especially around my chin and forehead. My friend recommended it, and I’ve already gone through three tubes—it works wonders! Whenever a pimple pops up, I apply a small amount, and within a day or two, the redness and swelling are noticeably reduced. My skin feels smoother, and breakouts heal much faster. It’s now my go-to product for clear skin!

Skin Type: Suitable for normal to oily skin because it is a gel-like texture

What it does:

♡ Effective Acne Treatment: The acne cream uses anti-inflammatory agent Ibuprofen Piconol (IPPN) and antibacterial agent Isopropyl Methylphenol (IPMP) to treat troubled skin and reduce acne.
♡ Transparent on Application: The cream becomes transparent once applied to pimples, making it discreet and easy to use.
♡ Refreshing Botanical Fragrance: It has a light, botanical fragrance that adds a pleasant touch to your skincare routine.

Key Ingredients:

⚝ Ibuprofen Piconol (IPPN) 3.0%: Reduces inflammation, helping to calm redness and swelling associated with acne.
⚝ Isopropyl Methylphenol (IPMP) 0.3%: Fights acne-causing bacteria, helping to prevent future breakouts.
⚝ Stearyl Alcohol: Moisturizes and helps stabilize the cream, making it smooth to apply.
⚝ Polysorbate 60: A surfactant that helps blend the ingredients together for a consistent formula.
